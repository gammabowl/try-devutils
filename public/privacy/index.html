TryDevUtils Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Summary

TryDevUtils is a local, privacy-first developer utility. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

What We Collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, or tracking data. All processing happens locally in your browser or extension.

Clipboard Access

Clipboard access is used only when you explicitly click copy or paste actions inside the extension. Clipboard contents are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Local Storage

If preferences are saved, they are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

Third Parties

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: hello@trydevutils.com
